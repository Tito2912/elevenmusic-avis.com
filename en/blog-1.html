Create Your First Music with ElevenMusic AI (Complete Guide)

Published March 15, 2025 â€¢ Reading time: 7 min

ElevenMusic by ElevenLabs revolutionizes music creation by allowing anyone to generate original tracks in seconds. This step-by-step guide will show you how to create your first composition, even without musical knowledge.

Step 1: Access ElevenMusic

Go to the official ElevenMusic website and log in (or create a free account). The free version already allows full testing.

Step 2: Understand the Interface

The studio consists of 3 main sections:

  • Music prompt: Describe your music
  • Advanced settings: Style, BPM, instruments
  • Preview: Listen and adjust
Pro tip: Start with simple prompts like "energetic pop music" before experimenting with complex descriptions.

Step 3: Your First Prompt

Try this prompt to start:

"An energetic electro pop music with bright synths and catchy melody, tempo 120 BPM, no lyrics"

Step 4: Advanced Customization

Explore the options:

Parameter Recommended values
Duration 30s to test, 2-3 min for a complete track
Style Pop, Electro, Cinematic, Lo-fi...
Instruments Synth, Piano, Guitar, Drums...

Step 5: Generation and Export

Click "Generate" (count 15-45 seconds). Once satisfied:

  1. Download the .mp3 or .wav file
  2. Share the link directly
  3. Integrate into your video projects

Practical case: Music for YouTube

For a powerful YouTube intro:

"Short dynamic intro with powerful beats and futuristic sound effects, 10 seconds, cyberpunk style"

Instant result, royalty-free for your channel.
